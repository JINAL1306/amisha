Here's a time line of Einstein's life:

  • 1879 - Born in Ulm in Germany
  • 1880 - The familiy moved to München, where he went to Catholic school, even though his father was a jew.
  • 1888 - Started at Luitpold Gymnasium. He resented the school's regimen and teaching method.
  • 1902 - He gets a daugther, Lieserl Einstein, with Mileva Marić. He probably never saw his daughter. Her fate after 1903 is uncertain. Letters to Marić suggest that the girl was either adopted or died of scarlet fever in infancy.
  • 1903 - Marries Mileva Marić
  • 1904 - His son Hans was born in Bern.
  • 1905 - Has been called Einstein's miracle year, he published four groundbreaking papers, on the photoelectric effect, Brownian motion, special relativity, and the equivalence of mass and energy (the famous equation E = mc2)
  • 1915 - General relativity is a theory of gravitation that was developed between 1907 and 1915
  • 1919 - Divorced Mileva, and married Elsa Löwenthal. She was a first cousin maternally and a second cousin paternally. The marriage remained childless.
  • 1921 - Gets Nobel Prize in Physics "for his services to Theoretical Physics, and especially for his discovery of the law of the photoelectric effect".
  • 1933 - Visiting the United States when Adolf Hitler came to power, being Jewish, did not go back to Germany.
  • 1939 - Endorsed a letter to President Franklin D. Roosevelt alerting him to the potential development of "extremely powerful bombs of a new type" and recommending that the U.S. begin similar research. This eventually led to what would become the Manhattan Project.
  • 1955 - dies at the age of 76.
